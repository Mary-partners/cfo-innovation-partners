About Us

Climate risk expertise, applied where it matters most

CFO Innovation Partners is a Kenya-based climate risk and advisory firm. We bring deep expertise in TCFD climate scenario analysis, physical risk quantification, and climate finance frameworks — and we channel that institutional-grade capability toward the people who need it most: smallholder farmers across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our woman-led, youth-led team combines climate finance advisory experience with agile innovation capacity. We don't just build technology — we build trust, working directly with farming communities to co-design solutions that fit real workflows and decision cycles.
